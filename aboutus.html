About Us

We are among the best colleges in Chhattisgarh

Shri Shankaracharya Technical Campus, an insignia among the technical institutions in the region is the first Autonomus College in Central India to be named after Adi-Shri Shankaracharya and blessed by all present Jagadgurus. This college was commissioned during the session 1999-2000 with the goal of providing undergraduate education in Engineering and Technology, a close interaction with industry, and a strong emphasis on research. The logo of the college highlights the Iconic line from the Vedas “ज्ञानदेव तू कैवल्यं” which means: Only through knowledge one attains liberation.
